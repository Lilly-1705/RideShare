About Us

Our website was created to solve a significant travel problem for students during semester breaks and holidays. We recognized that students often struggle to find cabs due to high demand and fixed timings. Booking individual cabs is not only costly but also contributes to increased traffic congestion and carbon emissions. With no efficient system in place to optimize group travel , we developed this platform to promote an eco-friendly, cost-effective, and secure way for students to travel to and from campus. By enabling students to share cabs, our system helps them split expenses and reduce their carbon footprint by minimizing the number of vehicles on the road.
